How do I create my profile?

1. Go to your profile page by clicking "View Profile"

2. Click "Edit Profile" button

3. Fill in your bio, sport, location, and other details

4. Upload a profile photo by clicking the camera icon

5. Click "Save Changes" to update your profile

How do I connect with other athletes?

1. Go to the "Explore" page to discover athletes

2. Click on any user's profile to view their details

3. Click the "Connect" button on their profile

4. Your connection request will be sent

How do I create a post?

1. Go to your Dashboard/Feed page

2. Use the "Create Post" form at the top

3. Write your content in the text area

4. Click "Post" to submit (posts require admin approval)

Why isn't my post showing up?

All posts require admin approval before they appear on the platform. This ensures quality content and prevents spam. Your post will be visible once an admin reviews and approves it.
